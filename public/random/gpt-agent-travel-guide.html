Weather heuristics (Nov–Dec)
  • North (Hanoi, Ha Long) — cool & pleasant; crisp light. Light layers.
  • Central (Hue → Da Nang → Hoi An → Nha Trang) — showers taper; keep a compact poncho.
  • South (HCMC, Mekong, Phu Quoc) — dry season; warm, low rain. SPF 50.
Work‑friendly bases
  • Da Nang (An Thuong) — beach + cafés + fiber.
  • HCMC (District 1/3 & Thao Dien) — co‑works & food miles.
  • Hanoi (Tay Ho) — lakeside, quieter, good coffee.
Transit rhythm
  • Prefer rail & scenic roads (Hai Van Pass).
  • Use short flights for North↔South hops.
  • Night trains double as lodging.
